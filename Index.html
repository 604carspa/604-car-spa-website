Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our mobile service.

Do I need to provide water and electricity?

Yes. For most services, we require access to an outdoor water spigot and a standard power outlet (120V) within 50 feet of the vehicle. If you cannot provide these utilities (e.g., apartment/condo parking), you must inform us in the booking form, and we will arrange to bring the necessary resources, which may slightly adjust the appointment time.

What areas in Metro Vancouver do you cover?

We primarily serve the Lower Mainland (604 area code), including Surrey, Langley, Vancouver, Richmond, Burnaby, Delta, and Coquitlam. Travel fees may apply for locations outside of our immediate service radius, but we will confirm this with your quote.

Do I need to be present during the detailing service?

No! We require you to be present at the start for a quick vehicle walk-around and to receive the final keys, but you are free to carry on with your day while we work. Payment is collected upon completion and your final sign-off.

What if my car is extremely dirty (e.g., pet hair, mud)?

We handle all levels of dirt! For excessively challenging vehicles (heavy pet hair, construction dust, bio-hazards), an excessive dirt fee (starting at $20) may be applied. We will always inform you of any surcharge before starting work.

604 Car Spa Service Agreement

This agreement outlines the terms and conditions for mobile detailing services provided by 604 Car Spa to customers in the Metro Vancouver (604) area, covering Cars, SUVs, Pickup Trucks, RVs, and Boats.

1. Booking, Fees, & Cancellations

  • Promotional Pricing: Prices for the Basic, Advanced, and Extreme packages reflect a limited-time promotional discount applied to the base rate.
  • Final Price: The price quoted is an estimate. The final price is determined upon physical inspection of the vehicle prior to work commencing, based on actual size, condition, and chosen add-ons (e.g., heavy pet hair, excessive mud, or biohazard). Note: Prices are based on standard Car/Sedan size. SUVs/Pickup Trucks incur a $20 size surcharge for the Basic ($119) package only. The Advanced ($169) and Extreme ($199) packages are all-inclusive for these vehicle types. RV and Marine services are quote-only.
  • Payment: Payment is due upon completion of the service via Cash or E-transfer.
  • Rescheduling & Cancellations: Rescheduling is free if notification is provided at least **24 hours** before the scheduled appointment. If the client cancels with less than 24 hours notice, a cancellation fee of $50 may be applied.

2. Service Requirements (Mobile Detailing)

  • Utilities: The client is REQUIRED to provide access to an outdoor water spigot and a standard power outlet (120V) within 50 feet of the service location to maintain our service efficiency and pricing. If the client is unable to provide either utility, they must note this during booking so 604 Car Spa can prepare accordingly.
  • Access: Client must ensure safe and legal access to the vehicle at the specified location. This includes being able to open all doors, the hood, and the trunk/cabin.
  • Parking: Client is responsible for providing adequate parking space for the service vehicle. For RVs/Boats, this includes sufficient working space.

3. Damage and Liability

  • Pre-existing Conditions: 604 Car Spa is not responsible for damage caused by pre-existing conditions, including but not limited to: heavily scratched paint/gel coat, loose trim, faulty electronics, or damage from biohazards or pests.
  • Personal Items: The client is responsible for removing all valuable or personal belongings from the vehicle prior to the service. We are not liable for any lost or damaged items.
  • Satisfaction Guarantee: We offer a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you are not satisfied with the quality of the service, you must notify us before payment is rendered so we can correct the issue immediately.
